Angina Bird Sanctuary

On the auspicious eve of new-year celebration is approaching, Angina Bird Sanctuary under Kumarganj block of South Dinajpur district waits to attract more tourists. More than 100 species of migratory birds use to come every year at Angina wetland during the advent of winter.

Danga Forest at Dusk

Owls live in a variety of habitats, ranging from deserts to forests, and including human habitations in most easily seen. Most owl species are nocturnal and adapted for hunting at dusk or in the dark.

Kaldighi, Gangarampur

Gangarampur is one of the cities and a municipality in Dakshin Dinajpur district in the state of West Bengal, India. Buniadpur is the headquarters of the Gangarampur subdivision. The city is located on the bank of river Punarbhaba. Gangarampur is one of the major cities connected through National Highway 512.

Dogachi Forest

Located about 6 km away from Balurghat, Dogachi is one of the best and biggest forest of Dakshin Dinajpur District. The nearest railway station is situated 3 km away from Dogachi. Dogachi can be reached through road by taking buses, taxis and private cars. The pristine natural beauty and wonderful forest area makes it a preferred picnic spot for the visitors.

Raghunathpur Forest

About 1 km from Balurghat, there is a small forest that is flocked by picnickers during winters and weekends. People enjoy the natural beauty of this place a lot. A forest bungalow is there to provide accommodation to the tourists who wish to stay and enjoy this forest area.

Sarongbari

Sarongbari is a famous picnic spot in Hili Block of Dakshin Dinajput. Hilli houses the International Checkpost of India-Bangladesh. Located about 25 km away from Balurghat Hili has a small forest named Sarongbari. Local people as well as tourist come here to organize picnic. Hili Panchayat Samiti looks after a small hut with drinking water facilities.

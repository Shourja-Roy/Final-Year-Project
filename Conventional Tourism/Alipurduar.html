Rasikbil

Rasikbil or Rasikbeel is a small lake situated in the Cooch Behar district of West Bengal, India, but nearest town is Kamakhyaguri. This lake attracts a lot of birds which make nests in the trees around the lake. The bird species which live in and around the lake includes cormorants, different varieties of storks, ibis, spoonbill, kingfisher, parrots, owl and many others.

Chilapata Wildlife Sanctuary

The Chilapata Forest is a dense forest near Jaldapara National Park in Dooars, Alipurduar district, West Bengal, India. It is about 20 km from Alipurduar, and just a few minutes away from Hasimara town.

Pukuri

Ganga Pukhuri is a large pond situated in Barkuriha of Nalbari district. This pond is about 7 km away from Nalbari. It is said to had been dug by Gangadhar, a Brahmin and thus it attained its name. Every year there is a festival held in Ganga Pukhuri on Ashok Astami and devotees throng here from different parts of the country to pay homages to departed souls.

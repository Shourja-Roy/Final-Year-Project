Purpose

In today's fast paced world we have our internet connections to serve the speed we need. But if notice a bit minutely, we will understand that a lot of our time gets unaccounted when searching for correct and authentic results while surfing through numerous sites. More over, the validation of the authenticacy of our required piece of information remains an unaddressed region. To cater this need of the hour, let us have the privilege to welcome you here!

In The Bengal Tourism Thesaurus, you will get the most validated handpicked information of the state of West Bengal at your fingertips, 'In One Go', 'At One Place'. It is the authentic, user friendly and to the point approach which have been meticulously kept in mind while developing this website.

So, be it planning for the vacations, searching for immediate contacts for health care institutes of even finding the best college around, we have every authentic bit of District-Wise information for you!

Offered Tourism Sections


Educational Tourism



This section is solely dedicated for providing authentic information of the best educational institutes from each district of West Bengal. Be it searching for top rated schools for your child or planning to get admitted in one of the top colleges or universities, we will ensure that you make the right choice for your future.

Medical Tourism



This section is dedicated for providing details of the best medical institutes from each district of West Bengal. Be it searching for immediate contact details of the best hospitals during a critical medical emergency or looking for a nursing home to cure your ailments, we will provide you with all you need.

Conventional Tourism



This section is solely dedicated for providing authentic information of the best tourist spots in a particular district of West Bengal. Be it planning for a long trip or be to spending a weekend away from the daily hectic life, we will ensure that you never miss the best places of visits while you are spending your leisure time.

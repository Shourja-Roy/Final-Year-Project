Suri Sadar Hospital

3.9 (154) · Government hospital Suri, West Bengal Open 24 hours Type: Government Address: Suri Post Office Road, Hospital Road, Suri, West Bengal - 731101 Phone: Distance from Suri Railway Station: 3.9kms (10 mins) Suri Sadar Hospital is a district hospital situated at Suri, the District Headquarters of Birbhum district, India. It is also one of the three Super Specialty Hospitals built in Birbhum district. Apart from people of Suri, people of neighbouring villages and towns (such as, Rajnagar, Mohammad Bazar, Purandarpur, Sainthia etc.) and even the people of neighbouring state Jharkhand also depend on this hospital. The total number of beds in this hospital is 520. During the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic, a coronavirus testing laboratory was established at the Suri Sadar Hospital.

Rampurhat Health District Hospital

3.5 (102) · Government hospital Rampurhut, West Bengal Open 24 hours Type: Government (Open 24 hours) Address: Rampurhat Government Medical College and District Hospital, Rampurhat, Birbhum, West Bengal – 731224 Phone: 03461-255285 / 03461-255102 Distance from Rampurhat Railway station: 2.5 kms (8 mins) Rampurhat Government Medical College and Hospital is a full-fledged tertiary referral Government Medical college. It was established in the year 2018. The college imparts the degree Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery (MBBS). Nursing and para-medical courses are also offered. The college is affiliated to West Bengal University of Health Sciences and is recognised by Medical Council of India. The hospital associated with the college is one of the largest hospitals in the Birbhum district. The selection to the college is done on the basis of merit through National Eligibility and Entrance Test. Yearly undergraduate student intake is 100 from the year 2019. The medical college is divided into two campuses, main campus beside SH 14(District Hospital, Super Speciality Hospital and Academic Block) and the other at Chowk Mandala(Hostels)
